How it works

Three simple steps
to knowing yourself

No complex forms. No overwhelm. Just a gentle daily check-in that adds up over time.

🌤️

Track your mood daily

A simple tap each morning tells NuraSense how you're feeling — happy, tired, anxious, energised. Takes under 10 seconds.

🩺

Log symptoms when they arise

Headache? Low energy? Stomach discomfort? Log it gently and non-judgementally whenever something feels off.

✨

Discover patterns over time

NuraSense surfaces soft, educational insights — like connections between sleep, mood, and how your body feels week to week.

Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Thoughtfully designed tools to help you listen to your body without overwhelm.

🌡️

Mood Tracking

A quick, intuitive emotion check-in every day. Choose from a spectrum of feelings — NuraSense remembers so you don't have to.

🩻

Symptom Check-ins

Log physical symptoms — fatigue, headaches, digestion, tension — whenever they appear. Simple, quick, and compassionate.

💡

Personal Insights

Gentle, non-medical observations about your patterns — like "you tend to feel better mid-week" or "your energy dips on low-sleep days."

📈

History & Trends

A beautiful, private timeline of your mood and symptoms. Scroll back through weeks and months to see your journey with clarity.

📊
🔒

Privacy-First Design

Your data stays on your device. No ads. No selling your health data. NuraSense is built to earn your trust every day.

Trust & Transparency

Built on honesty

We believe wellness tools should be clear about what they are — and what they're not.

🛡️

Your data, your control

All your mood and symptom data is stored locally on your device. We never sell, share, or monetize your personal health information.

🤝

Informational only

NuraSense provides general wellness insights for educational purposes. We surface patterns — not diagnoses. You remain in full control of your health decisions.

Always seek professional care

For any medical concern, we encourage you to speak with a qualified healthcare professional. NuraSense is a companion, not a replacement for medical advice.
